# Sprint Adjustments

> **Closing-artifact bias:** `sprint-adjustments` (mode: `sprint`).
> Drafted at CLOSE — feeds the facilitator's notes-drafting prompt.

## Story injections

New stories surfaced and proposed for the active sprint. Each row carries an
intended priority and an effort estimate.

- <story-key proposal> — <one-sentence summary> — priority P? — size S/M/L.

## Scope cuts

Stories proposed for removal from the active sprint and their justification.

- <story-key> — reason for cut.

## Priority shifts

Stories whose priority changed during the meeting.

- <story-key> — old priority → new priority — reason.

## Follow-ups

- `sprint-correction` action items feeding `/gaia-correct-course`.
- `new-story` action items feeding `/gaia-create-story`.
- `sprint-plan` action items feeding `/gaia-sprint-plan`.
