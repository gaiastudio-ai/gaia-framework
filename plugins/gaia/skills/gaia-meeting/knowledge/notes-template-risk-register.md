## Notes

- Likelihood and Impact are 3-state estimates (low / medium / high). Reserve
  fine-grained quantitative scoring for a follow-up DREAD pass via
  `/gaia-threat-model` if the meeting surfaces a security-class risk.
- Each row's mitigation must be testable — "improve X" is not a mitigation.
- Follow-up `risk-followup` action item candidates carry remaining mitigations.
