# Decision Record

> **Closing-artifact bias:** `decision-record` (mode: `decide`).
> Drafted at CLOSE — fed into the SAVE-time meeting notes file as the
> facilitator's notes-drafting prompt.

## Decision

State the decision in one sentence. Use the active voice ("Adopt X" / "Defer Y
to next sprint").

## Context

Why was this decision needed? What problem space framed it?

## Alternatives considered

- Option A — short rationale.
- Option B — short rationale.
- Option C — short rationale.

## Consequences

- Positive consequence 1.
- Negative consequence 1.
- Follow-up actions captured as `decision-followup` action items.

## Owner

Name the owner who carries the decision forward.
