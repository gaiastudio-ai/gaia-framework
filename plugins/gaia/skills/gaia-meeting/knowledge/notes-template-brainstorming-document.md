# Idea Clusters

> **Closing-artifact bias:** `brainstorming-document` (mode: `brainstorm`).
> Drafted at CLOSE — feeds the facilitator's notes-drafting prompt.

## Idea clusters

Group surfaced ideas into thematic clusters. Each cluster gets a short label
and 2-5 bullet ideas.

- **Cluster A — <label>**
  - Idea 1
  - Idea 2
  - Idea 3

- **Cluster B — <label>**
  - Idea 1
  - Idea 2

## Ranked options

After clustering, rank the most viable options. Use a 1-line rationale per
ranked entry.

1. <option> — rationale.
2. <option> — rationale.
3. <option> — rationale.

## Follow-ups

- `brainstorm-followup` action items consumable by `/gaia-brainstorm` for
  deeper exploration of any cluster or ranked option.
