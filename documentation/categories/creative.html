Creative & Collaboration

Creative and collaboration commands run multi-agent sessions for brainstorming, design thinking, problem solving, presentations, and stakeholder discussions. They produce artifacts in .gaia/artifacts/creative-artifacts/.

Multi-agent sessions

  • /gaia-party user-facing Multi-agent group discussion with dynamic participant selection (Party Mode).
  • /gaia-meeting user-facing Peer-to-peer multi-agent discussion with charter, round-robin turn arbitration, and phase lifecycle.
  • /gaia-brainstorming user-facing Facilitated brainstorming session with structured ideation phases.
  • /gaia-create-stakeholder user-facing Create a new stakeholder persona file for use in Party Mode discussions.

Design and strategy

  • /gaia-design-thinking user-facing Guided human-centered design session through the five Stanford d.school phases.
  • /gaia-creative-sprint user-facing Multi-agent creative pipeline: empathize, solve, innovate -- producing a unified creative brief.
  • /gaia-problem-solving user-facing Apply systematic problem-solving methodologies with resolution routing.
  • /gaia-innovation user-facing Identify disruption opportunities with structured innovation strategy analysis.
  • /gaia-adversarial user-facing Adversarial review session that stress-tests ideas by arguing opposing viewpoints.

Presentations and narrative

  • /gaia-storytelling user-facing Craft a compelling narrative using story frameworks and emotional design.
  • /gaia-pitch-deck user-facing Create an investor or partner pitch deck with standard business slide structure.
  • /gaia-slide-deck user-facing Create a presentation slide deck with narrative arc, visual specs, and speaker notes.
