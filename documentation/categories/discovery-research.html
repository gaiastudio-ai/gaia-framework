Discovery & Research

These commands help you explore ideas, research markets and technologies, and synthesize findings into a product brief. They are typically the first commands you run after /gaia-init, corresponding to Phase 1 (Analysis) of the GAIA lifecycle.

Commands in this category

  • /gaia-discover user-facing Manage the Discovery Board -- a pre-backlog staging area for capturing, researching, evaluating, and graduating ideas before they enter the formal lifecycle via /gaia-add-feature.
  • /gaia-brainstorm user-facing Brainstorm a new project idea through guided creative exploration.
  • /gaia-product-brief user-facing Create a product brief covering vision, users, problem, solution, scope, risks, and success metrics.
  • /gaia-market-research user-facing Conduct market research to understand competitors, market size, and positioning.
  • /gaia-domain-research user-facing Research a domain or industry -- key players, regulations, trends, and terminology.
  • /gaia-tech-research user-facing Evaluate and compare technologies with objective trade-off analysis before architecture decisions.
  • /gaia-advanced-elicitation user-facing Deep requirements elicitation using structured techniques (5 Whys, Kano Model, Jobs-to-be-Done, and more).
